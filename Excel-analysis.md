## Excel Financial Analysis

### Overview  
This section analyzes the financial performance of Apple, Microsoft, and Nvidia using revenue, operating margins, and free cash flow. The goal is to evaluate growth, profitability, and scalability across companies.

---

### Revenue Trends  

- Apple shows steady and consistent revenue growth over the period
- Microsoft demonstrates moderate growth with stable expansion
- Nvidia shows minimal growth from 2022 to 2023, followed by explosive growth in 2024 and 2025

**Nvidia’s revenue trend indicates a major demand shift rather than a gradual expansion.**

---

### Revenue Growth  

- Apple and Microsoft maintain relatively stable growth rates  
- Nvidia experiences near-zero growth in 2023, followed by growth exceeding 100% in 2024 and 2025  

**This reflects a transition from stable performance to hyper-growth.**

---

### Operating Margin Analysis  

- Apple and Microsoft maintain strong and consistent operating margins  
- Nvidia experiences a decline in 2023 before significantly increasing in 2024 and 2025  

**This indicates strong operating leverage and improved profitability during periods of high demand.**

---

### Free Cash Flow Trends  

- Apple and Microsoft generate stable and consistent free cash flow  
- Nvidia shows a decline in 2023, followed by a sharp increase in 2024 and 2025  

**This suggests reinvestment followed by strong cash generation as revenue scaled.**

---

### Free Cash Flow Margin  

- Apple and Microsoft maintain stable cash flow efficiency  
- Nvidia’s FCF margin declines in 2023 and then increases significantly  

**This highlights improved efficiency and scalability in later years.**

---

### Takeaways  

- Apple and Microsoft represent stable, consistent performers with moderate growth  
- Nvidia demonstrates high-growth potential with significant profitability expansion  
- Nvidia’s performance reflects a shift from stable operations to rapid expansion driven by increased demand  
- Strong margin expansion suggests scalability and pricing power  

**Overall, Nvidia presents a high-growth profile, while Apple and Microsoft provide more stable and predictable financial performance.**
