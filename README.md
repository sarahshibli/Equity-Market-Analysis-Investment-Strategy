# Equity Research & Portfolio Allocation Project

## Overview
This project analyzes the financial and market performance of major technology companies—Apple, Microsoft, and Nvidia—relative to the S&P 500 index. The analysis focuses on evaluating company fundamentals, market behavior, and investment potential using both financial modeling and data analytics.

## Objective
- Evaluate company performance using financial metrics and market data
- Analyze trends in revenue growth, profitability, and stock returns
- Assess risk and return characteristics across companies
- Develop a data-driven portfolio allocation strategy and investment recommendation

## Companies Analyzed
- Apple
- Microsoft
- Nvidia

## Benchmark
- S&P 500 Index

## Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel (financial modeling & valuation)
- Python (data analysis & stock performance)
- Tableau (optional: data visualization)

## Analysis Includes
- Revenue growth and profitability analysis (operating and net margins)
- Stock return, volatility, and correlation analysis
- Portfolio risk-return modeling and performance comparison
- Discounted cash flow (DCF) valuation
- Visualizations of financial and market trends

## Expected Outcome
- Identification of performance differences across companies
- Insights into growth trends, profitability, and risk profiles
- Valuation-based assessment (undervalued, fairly valued, or overvalued)
- Final investment recommendation (Buy, Hold, or Sell)
- Portfolio allocation strategy based on risk-return analysis
